Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can a 3rd-year B.Tech student apply for GATE 2027?

A: Yes, the updated eligibility criteria allows students currently in the 3rd year or higher of any undergraduate degree program to apply for GATE 2027. This is a significant relaxation from previous years.

Q: What is the minimum educational qualification for GATE CS?

A: Candidates must have completed or be pursuing a bachelor's degree in Engineering, Technology, Architecture, Science, Commerce, Arts, or Humanities from a government-approved university or institution.

Q: How many times can I attempt GATE CS?

A: There is no limit on the number of attempts. You can appear for GATE every year as long as you meet the eligibility criteria at the time of application.
